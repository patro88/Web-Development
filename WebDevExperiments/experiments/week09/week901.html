Project: Home Page design

Description:

In this week, I started working further with my project. I have tried to work on a home page design here. The home page design is taken from the bootstrap example of navbar. There are tab representing different functionality without user login requirement. These are Questions, Tags, Users, Ask Questions and About me tabs.

There is also a sign in tab at the right end of the nav bar. This design was chosen by me as I thought of making a carousal page in the middle and a fixed header at top.
